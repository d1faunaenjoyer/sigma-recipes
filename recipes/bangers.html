Bangers and Mash

A British "pub classic". Bangers and Mash is a staple in every pub known to man consisting of pork sausages (the bangers), mashed potatoes, peas, and onion gravy.

This recipe is simple and simultaneously good. Let's keep it that way with this recipe.

Notes:

  • If you are Muslim, you can substitute the pork sausage for a Halal meat of your own choice
  • The sausage part of this recipe cannot be avoided. Please use a sausage of some sort.
  • I heavily recommend that you buy fresh peas for this dish.
